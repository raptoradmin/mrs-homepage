Roof restoration is an extremely viable option for many building owners, especially those in need of immediate repairs. Poor design and workmanship account for about 90 percent of all roof failures! Laying over a wet roof, not replacing perimeter sheet metal and not providing adequate provision for drainage are some of the most common errors. It is crucial to your roof’s performance to select a thorough, knowledgeable contractor and hire roofers that can deliver on their promises to protect your business investment. While a new roof is also an excellent investment, it isn’t always necessary because it adds significant cost to the roofing project and may not be necessary to fix the problems in compliance with your counties code regulations. Leave to the roofing experts at Mountain Roofing Systems to restore your building’s roof with quality products, using experienced roofers to add a professional touch to your family’s investment.
